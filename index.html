How this site was created?

2023-04-15

Web Design HTML CSS Javascript

This side was create with HTML, CSS and Javascript



I used a grid-based layout with a header, left sidebar, main content area, and right sidebar, with styles that create a modern appearance.


Features


1. Search bar


The search bar is the first thing I wanted to make for this website. The search icon highlights upon clicking, and an 'X' button allows for the removal of typed text.

search
clear

2. Icon and navigation bar



I incorporate internet fonts, such as Google Fonts and Font Awesome icons, and have ensured responsive adjustments are in place.


3. Responsive



I carefully designed the responsive, hard to put it in words. There still bugs when you dramatically resize the site in the extreme way, I still don't know how to fix, maybe it trigger something that I can't find out.


To fix the responsive issue if occur, you can reload the site, it fixes most cases.


P/S: On the right sidebar, when you open up an archive, if it too long, you can still scroll if separately, I hidden its scrollbar.


4. Functions



You can "heart" the article by click on the "heard". The counter will increase, and when you click again, it back to previous state.


You can click on "Show more" to show more contents, or just click to "Contents" on the left sidebar. The site will scroll to the first article showed up.


5. Animations



I wanted to add more animation, but the code is messed up when I try adding it. I tried my best on "Top Articles", struggled with the CSS and Javascript for it very much.


6. Forms


I have two forms of login and sign up, each have validation as well.

Streamer abandoned his viewers to play game overnight with his wife

2023-04-16

Entertainment Gaming


Once upon a time, there was a famous streamer who had a loyal following of viewers. He was known for his witty banter, engaging gameplay, and charming personality. One evening, as he was streaming his latest game, his wife came into the room and asked if he wanted to play together.


The streamer hesitated for a moment, glancing at his computer screen and the chat full of enthusiastic viewers. But then he remembered how long it had been since he and his wife had spent quality time together, and he knew what he had to do. He abandoned his viewers, promising to be back soon, and logged off.


What followed was an epic gaming session, as the streamer and his wife played into the wee hours of the morning. They laughed, joked, and competed against each other, completely lost in the world of the game. The streamer forgot all about his viewers, until he woke up the next morning to a flood of angry messages and comments.


Feeling guilty, the streamer quickly apologized to his viewers, promising to make it up to them with an extra-long stream the next day. But as he looked back on the night before, he couldn't help but smile at the memories he had made with his wife. He realized that sometimes, it's worth abandoning your fans for a little bit to focus on the people who matter most.
